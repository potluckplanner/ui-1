Potluck Planner

Like Stone Soup, potlucks are a great idea in theory: everyone brings a dish to share, and you end up with a feast. In practice, however, they can be hard to manage. What if the dishes clash? What if there are no beverages or ice or bowls or... dessert?! In this digital world of social gatherings and casual dining, Potluck Planner does away with coordinating text messages, online to-do lists and spreadsheets so that you can sit with friends and share a meal. Its your one-stop-shop for food with friends!

We make it so you don't have to guess what your guests will bring..

Let's Plan!

Potluck Planner is an easy to use app that allows hosts to manage all aspects of their potluck from the comfort of their homes. Users are able to connect with friends, schedule potlucks, invite guests, manage potential potluck menus and monitor RSVPs all in one app. And user experience doesn't just end there, users are able to flex between the role of host and guest with ease. While using Potluck Planner's Dishlist, hosts can compile a list of potluck staples for guests to choose to bring when submitting their RSVP. Guests have the ability to add items to the Dishlist as needed. Whether you're planning a wonderful one off potluck, or a standing engagement, let Potluck Planner help you one dish at a time.

Features

Create Personalized Accounts

With the ability to log in and log out of Potluck Planner, you'll never lose track of your events again! Users have the ability to not only host events but also attend them as guests. Our app has versitility that adds to the ease of use of users. Look for features like Dietary Restriction Log, Typical Availability & Location Preference in the near future

Flexible Scheduling

Our software allows you to create and delete upcoming potlucks with ease! Gone are the days of last minute cancellations and nonexsistant rainchecks. Potluck Planner allows users the flexibility to schedule events, modify potluck details, or simply delete them if necessary. Look for intuitive scheduling assistant & auto raincheck intergration in the near future

Make a Menu "Dishlist"

Potluck Planner makes it so you don't have to guess what your guests will bring. With the ability to create an itemized list of potluck staples and assign dishes to specific guests, you no longer have to choke your way through a friend's newest "spin" on a classic. Look for the abilitiy to save, share and send favorited Dishlists to other users for easy access

Invite, RSVP, Modify

Guests can confirm their attendace though our app and edit their contributions. Gone are the days of having to keep track of 30 different text threads to ensure that your friends will be coming to your potluck. Potluck Planner allows users to RSVP on the spot and modify said RSVP as needed. Look for calender integration, notifications, and reminders in futher app updates
